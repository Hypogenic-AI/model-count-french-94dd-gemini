# Downloaded Papers

1.  **[Probing for Multilingual Numerical Understanding in Transformer-Based Language Models](2020.blackboxnlp-1.18_Johnson_Probing_Multilingual_Numerical.pdf)**
    - Authors: Johnson et al.
    - Year: 2020
    - Why relevant: Shows that older transformer models (BERT-era) struggle with magnitude estimation, especially in French's vigesimal system.

2.  **[Investigating the interaction of linguistic and mathematical reasoning in language models using multilingual number puzzles](2506.13886_Bhattacharya_2025_Multilingual_Number_Puzzles.pdf)**
    - Authors: Bhattacharya et al.
    - Year: 2025
    - Why relevant: Shows that even SOTA models fail at "implicit math" in numeral systems.

3.  **[mOthello: When Do Cross-Lingual Representation Alignment and Cross-Lingual Transfer Emerge in Multilingual Models?](2404.12444_Hua_2024_mOthello_Cross_Lingual.pdf)**
    - Authors: Hua et al.
    - Year: 2024
    - Why relevant: Background on how multilingual models align representations.

4.  **[Type and Complexity Signals in Multilingual Question Representations](2510.06304_QTC_Dataset.pdf)**
    - Authors: Kokot & Poelman
    - Year: 2025
    - Why relevant: Complexity signals in multilingual representations.

5.  **[DistilCamemBERT: a distillation of the French model CamemBERT](2205.11111_Delestre_2022_DistilCamemBERT.pdf)**
    - Authors: Delestre & Amar
    - Year: 2022
    - Why relevant: Context on French-specific BERT models.
